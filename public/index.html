01

Team rules & scripts
Language rules — what your team says
Never say
"This is the lowest price of the day"
Always say
"Best we've seen today — so far"
Never say
"Price won't go lower"
Always say
"Prices are moving live. No fixed direction."
Never say
"Next drop in X minutes"
Always say
"No idea when — or if — it changes next"
Never confirm
The floor price exists or what it is
After a spike say
"It was lower earlier. Not sure if it returns."
Drop mechanics
Floor rule
Floor is NEVER the last drop. Always spike after.
Window rule
Once a price is revealed, members have exactly 15 minutes to book. Timer resets on every drop.
Early bird rule
If you run the sunrise flash, never promise it returns later in the day.
Silence rule
At least one silence window per day. Post "no active drop" as a story. Silence is content.
Instagram cadence
Every price drop
Story within 3 minutes. Use pre-built template. Change price only.
Every spike
Story within 60 seconds: "price just went up." DM all warm leads immediately.
End of day
Post "day closed" story. Show best price (do not call it the floor). Seeds tomorrow's attention.
Hot lead DMs
After every floor hit: personalised DM. "Caught it? It just went up." No generic blasts.
